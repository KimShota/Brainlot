# Privacy Policy Template for Brainlot

## Introduction

Brainlot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Collected during registration for account creation and authentication
- **Authentication Data**: When using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we receive your name and email
- **User ID**: A unique identifier assigned to your account

### Usage Data
- **Upload Statistics**: Number of files uploaded and timestamps
- **Subscription Information**: Your plan type (Free or Pro) and subscription status
- **Quiz Performance**: Scores and progress on generated quizzes (stored locally on your device)

### File Data
- **Uploaded Files**: PDFs and images you upload are temporarily processed by our AI service (Google Gemini) to generate quiz questions
- **Storage**: Files are NOT permanently stored on our servers. They are processed in memory and discarded after quiz generation
- **Content**: We do not read, analyze, or use your uploaded content for any purpose other than generating quizzes for you

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Create and manage your account
- Generate AI-powered quiz questions from your study materials
- Enforce upload limits (10 files for Free users, unlimited for Pro users)
- Process subscription payments (if applicable)
- Respond to support requests
- Improve our app and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- **Database**: User data is stored securely on Supabase servers
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Authentication**: We use industry-standard OAuth 2.0 for authentication
- **File Processing**: Uploaded files are processed in memory and never saved to disk on our servers
- **Local Storage**: Quiz progress and scores may be cached locally on your device
